About

This car is part of the C-Class series from Mercedes-Benz. Here are some general specifications of the Mercedes C180: - Engine: The C180 comes with a 1.6-liter engine producing 154 horsepower. - Performance: The C180 accelerates from 0 to 100 kilometers per hour in about 8.5 seconds and has a top speed of around 223 kilometers per hour. - Driving System: The C180 uses rear-wheel drive, providing optimal weight distribution and a sporty driving experience. - Design: The C180 features an elegant and stylish design, with sleek lines and attractive curves. It is available in a variety of colors and has unique details such as a wide front grille and dual exhausts. - Technology: The C180 is equipped with advanced technologies, including a navigation system, high-quality sound system, and a touchscreen display.
