HYUNDAI Elantra 2022

About Car:

         The 2022 Hyundai Elantra features distinctive exterior specifications that make it stand out with elegance and appeal.
        The front includes LED reflector lamps and the rear LED, giving it advanced and prominent illumination in the dark. The
        car's design is characterized by a sharp, triangular geometric language, which adds a touch of elegance and distinction
         to its exterior appearance.
            
        The front has a matte black grille that adds an attractive and sporty touch to the front. Full LED DRL headlights are
         available, enhancing visibility and making driving safe in all conditions. The car is also equipped with electrically
        folding side mirrors for convenience and ease of use.
            
        As for the 18-inch light-alloy wheels, they add a touch of elegance and sportiness to the side of the car. All of this
        aesthetic design combines to give the 2022 Hyundai Elantra an exceptional exterior appearance that easily outperforms
        competitors in its class.
